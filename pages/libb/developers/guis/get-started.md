With Libb, you can create GUIs of any complexity using simple methods.

1. [AdvancedGui](/libb/developers/guis/advancedgui) – A basic GUI builder: items via `ItemWrapper`, click handling, and slot locking—without the hassle of dealing with standard Bukkit Inventories.
2. [PaginatedGui](/libb/developers/guis/paginatedgui) – Same as `AdvancedGui`, but with pagination support.
3. [ParsedGui](/libb/developers/guis/parsedgui) – Assembles a ready-to-use `PaginatedGui` directly from a YAML file; it allows you to define custom assembly rules, as well as create custom placeholders and click actions throughout the GUI.
